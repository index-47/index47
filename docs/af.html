AF
AF | Alliance First is a term used when a team advances to the next competition level by being on the first-place winning alliance during elimination rounds. At the end of an FTC event, the alliance that wins finals earns advancement spots for its member teams. Getting picked by a strong alliance captain and winning eliminations together is one of the main ways teams advance without needing a judged award — which is why scouting, strategy, and building a robot that complements other robots are all critical parts of the FTC experience.
