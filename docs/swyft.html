SWYFT
Swyft Robotics is an FTC-focused parts supplier that specializes in high-performance custom components designed to give teams a competitive edge. They offer products like lightweight custom drivetrain plates, optimized intake rollers, specialty hardware, and precision-machined brackets. Swyft parts are popular among advanced teams looking for premium components that go beyond what standard build systems offer — filling the gap between off-the-shelf kits and fully custom-machined solutions.
