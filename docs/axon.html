AXON
Axon Robotics is an FTC parts supplier known for their high-performance programmable servos. Their flagship products — the Axon MAX+ and Axon Mini — offer significantly more torque and speed than stock REV or goBILDA servos, with built-in programmable features like adjustable speed profiles and position feedback. Teams use Axon servos for high-demand mechanisms like active intakes, wrist joints, and claw pivots where a standard servo would stall or be too slow. They've become increasingly popular among competitive teams looking for a servo upgrade without going full custom.
