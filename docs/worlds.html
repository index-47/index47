WORLDS
Worlds is the FIRST World Championship — the final and highest level of FTC competition. Held annually in Houston, Texas, it brings together the best teams from every state and country. The event spans multiple days and includes qualification matches, judging, alliance selection, and a massive elimination bracket. Teams qualify for Worlds by winning advancing awards or the winning alliance at their State Championship. It also features an expo hall, workshops, and ceremonies across all FIRST programs (FLL, FTC, FRC). Making it to Worlds is considered the ultimate achievement in FTC.
