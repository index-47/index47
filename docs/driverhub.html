DRIVER HUB
Driver Hub is the driver station device made by REV Robotics that replaced the old Driver Station phone. It's a dedicated Android-based touchscreen device that runs the FTC Driver Station app. Drivers plug two gamepads into it via USB, and it communicates with the Control Hub over Wi-Fi Direct. The Driver Hub handles OpMode selection, gamepad input mapping, match start/stop controls, and displays telemetry data from the robot. Every FTC team needs one — it sits on the driver station shelf during matches.
