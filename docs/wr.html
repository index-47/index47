WR
WR | World Record is the highest match score ever recorded for a particular FTC game season. World records are tracked across all official events globally and are a big deal in the community. Teams chase WRs by maximizing autonomous scoring, optimizing cycle times in TeleOp, and nailing endgame tasks consistently. Scores are tracked on sites like FTC Scout and The Orange Alliance. Holding a WR — even briefly — is a huge flex and puts a team on the map.
