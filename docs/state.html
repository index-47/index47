STATES
States is the State Championship level of FTC competition. It brings together the top teams from across an entire state (or equivalent region) who advanced through Qualifiers and Regionals. This is where teams compete for a spot at the FIRST World Championship. The stakes are high — robot performance, engineering notebook quality, outreach, and GP all matter. In large states like Texas and California, the State Championship can have 50+ teams and is often one of the most competitive events of the entire season outside of Worlds itself!
