REV
REV | REV Robotics is a major FTC parts supplier and the official provider of the Control Hub and Expansion Hub , which are the core electronics that run every FTC robot. REV also sells motors, servos, sensors, structural channel (25mm extrusion), wheels, and build systems. Their ecosystem uses M3 hardware and 8mm REX shafts. REV products are known for being affordable and well-documented, making them a go-to choice for many FTC teams — especially newer ones building their first competitive robot.
