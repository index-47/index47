CONTROL AWARD
Control Award is an FTC judged award that recognizes the team with the most innovative and efficient control system on their robot. Judges look at the use of sensors, software algorithms, and carefully engineered feedback loops. Teams that win this award typically demonstrate things like custom PID tuning, computer vision pipelines, advanced autonomous routines, and well-documented code. To be considered, teams need to fill out a separate Control Award sheet explaining their software and sensor strategy. This award can advance teams to the next competition level.
