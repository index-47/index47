QUALIFIER
Qualifier is the first level of official FTC competition in a season. Teams attend one or more qualifier events where they play qualification matches, go through judging, and compete in elimination rounds. Teams can advance to the next level (Regionals or States depending on the region) by winning the Inspire Award, winning as part of the first-place alliance, or receiving other advancing awards. Most regions run multiple qualifiers throughout the season, and teams can typically attend more than one to improve their chances of advancing.
