DEAN'S LIST
Dean's List is an individual student award in FTC named after Dean Kamen, the founder of FIRST. Each team can nominate up to two 10th or 11th grade students who show outstanding leadership, dedication to spreading STEM in their community, and a strong commitment to the FIRST mission. Nominees are interviewed by judges at events, and semi-finalists advance through state and regional levels. Winners are announced at the FIRST World Championship and become Dean's List Finalists — one of the most prestigious individual honors in all of FIRST.
